CryptoCurrency: Future Of India

Cryptocurrency is a form of digital money which is renowned for its secure nature and anonymity. It is an internet-based medium of exchange which uses cryptographical functions to conduct financial transactions. Cryptocurrencies leverage blockchain technology to gain decentralization ,transparency, and immutability.

About CommonCoin

CommonCoin is a cryptocurrency developed to encourage usage of cashless modes of money tranfer and for the increase of decentralized and transparent money circulation. You will be given a fixed amount of commoncoins when you create your account. You can transact with your fellow members in the commoncoin community through this website. You will also be able to view your transaction history whenever required.
